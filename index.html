🔒 Privacy Policy

Money Manager Pro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025  |  Developed by Ullasan KV, Kerala

1. Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your name, phone number, email address and city. All financial data you enter (transactions, loans, goals) is stored only on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your registration details are used solely to understand our user base and improve the app. We do not sell, share or disclose your personal information to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your financial data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. Registration information is stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud). Your data is never used for advertising.

4. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. You can clear all local data from Settings → Clear All Data.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. Financial data never leaves your device.
